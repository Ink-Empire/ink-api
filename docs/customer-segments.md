# Inked In: Customer Segments

This document outlines the primary customer segments for Inked In and the key needs we aim to address for each group. Our platform is designed to serve the entire tattoo ecosystem by connecting clients, artists, and studios through a location-aware discovery and appointment scheduling platform.

## Prospective Clients

**Profile:** Individuals with few or no tattoos who are exploring the possibility of getting their first tattoo.

**Key Needs:**
- Education about tattoo styles, processes, and aftercare
- Ability to browse artists' portfolios filtered by style preferences
- Location-based discovery of nearby artists and studios
- Cost estimation and consultation scheduling
- Safety and reputation verification tools

**Platform Features:**
- Style guides and educational content
- Artist portfolios with style filtering
- Geographic search capabilities
- In-app consultation scheduling
- Review systems and artist verification
- Upload tattoos to their profile and tag the artist who did the work
- Public user profile page showing their uploaded tattoos

## Experienced Collectors

**Profile:** Tattoo enthusiasts with multiple pieces who understand the industry and are looking for specific artists or styles.

**Key Needs:**
- Advanced search for specific artists and styles
- Appointment scheduling and management
- Travel planning around artist availability
- Tracking and showcasing their personal collection
- Communication with artists about custom work

**Platform Features:**
- Advanced filtering by style, technique, and specialty
- Calendar synchronization and appointment notifications
- "Bucket list" artist tracking with availability alerts
- Personal collection gallery with uploaded tattoos and artist attribution
- Secure artist-client messaging system
- Discoverable via user search when artist search returns no results

## Tattoo Artists

**Profile:** Professional tattoo artists looking to showcase their work, manage their client relationships, and grow their business.

**Key Needs:**
- Portfolio showcasing and curation
- Client discovery and acquisition
- Appointment scheduling and management
- Reputation building through reviews
- Professional networking with other artists and studios

**Platform Features:**
- Customizable portfolio pages
- Search visibility optimization
- Integrated calendar and booking management
- Review collection and highlighting tools
- Artist-to-artist networking features

## Traveling Artists

**Profile:** Artists who guest spot at different studios, participate in conventions, or travel for specific clients.

**Key Needs:**
- Discovery of guest spot opportunities
- Promoting travel dates and locations
- Temporary appointment scheduling
- Managing client relationships across locations
- Building relationships with studios in different areas

**Platform Features:**
- Guest spot listings and searches
- Travel schedule announcements
- Location-specific availability management
- Global client database
- Studio relationship management tools

## Tattoo Studios

**Profile:** Business owners who manage physical tattoo locations with multiple artists.

**Key Needs:**
- Studio brand promotion
- Artist recruitment and management
- Collective portfolio showcase
- Coordinated scheduling across artists
- Business analytics and growth insights

**Platform Features:**
- Studio profile pages and branding tools
- Artist management dashboard
- Unified studio portfolio
- Integrated multi-artist scheduling
- Performance analytics and reporting

## Implementation Priorities

Our development roadmap focuses on building features that serve multiple customer segments simultaneously:

1. **Core Search & Discovery** - Geographic and style-based search serving all user types
2. **Portfolio Management** - Enabling artists and studios to showcase their work
3. **Appointment Scheduling** - Streamlining the booking process between clients and artists
4. **User Relationships** - Building systems for favoriting, following, and communications
5. **Travel & Availability** - Supporting the mobile nature of artists and clients

By addressing these priorities, we aim to create a platform that connects the tattoo community, makes quality tattoo art more accessible, and helps artists and studios build sustainable businesses.
